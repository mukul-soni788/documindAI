Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond to support requests?

We aim to respond to all support requests within 2 hours during business hours and within 24 hours on weekends. Enterprise customers receive priority support with guaranteed response times.

Do you offer custom integrations?

Yes! We offer custom API integrations and can work with your development team to integrate DocuMind AI into your existing workflows. Contact our sales team to discuss your specific requirements.

Is there a free trial available?

We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to get started. You can process up to 100 documents during your trial period.

What file formats do you support?

We support PDF, DOCX, DOC, TXT, JPG, JPEG, PNG, and TIFF files. We're constantly adding support for new formats based on user feedback.

How secure is my data?

Security is our top priority. We use enterprise-grade encryption, comply with SOC 2 Type II standards, and are GDPR compliant. Your documents are processed securely and deleted after processing unless you choose to store them.
