Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan at any time?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing adjustments on your next invoice.

What happens if I exceed my document limit?

If you exceed your monthly document limit, you can either upgrade to a higher plan or purchase additional documents at $0.10 per document. We'll notify you before you reach your limit.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial for all paid plans. You get full access to all features during the trial period, and no credit card is required to start.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied with our service, contact us within 30 days for a full refund.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for enterprise customers. All payments are processed securely through Razorpay.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption, comply with SOC 2 Type II standards, and are GDPR compliant. Your documents are processed securely and deleted after processing unless you choose to store them.
